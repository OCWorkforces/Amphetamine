Amphetamine is a macOS tray app that keeps your Mac awake. It runs on Electron 42 and TypeScript, uses `powerSaveBlocker` for sleep prevention, and supports timed or indefinite sessions with a battery-based auto-off. The build pipeline (Rslib for main/preload, Rsbuild for renderer) outputs CJS from ESM source; packaging produces a notarization-exempt DMG with Electron fuses flipped post-build.
